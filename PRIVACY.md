# Privacy Policy

**Reloom** is committed to protecting your privacy. This application is designed from the ground up to be **100% local**, **open-source**, and **private by default**.

## Local First & Zero Backend
- **No Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, analytics, or usage statistics.
- **No Backend:** This app does not communicate with any external servers to store your data. All of your connections, notes, places, and settings are saved securely on your device.
- **Full Ownership:** You have full ownership and control over your data.

## Open Source
Because the source code is openly available, anyone can independently verify our privacy claims. 

Your data belongs to you, and we intend to keep it that way.
